Introduction

We all commute the busy roads of where we live. With so many hazards on the road we want to always be informed and prepared for any and all conditions that we may come across in our vehicles while driving alone or even with our families. We decided to create a site that can help alleviate some of the burdens of the road: those indistinguishable and confusing road signs.

By taking a quick pictures safely while driving (preferably a passenger) you can log on to this site and upload the photo and in seconds it will let you know what the sign is trying to convey.
